Added: 21.07.19

Hello! ~

Welcome to the page of an artist who once got tired of trying to choose in which way personal portfolio would look like. PDF was definedly not my case, side site constructors are somewhat dissapointing since one of my directions and interests are website layouts. DeviantArt frustrates me even more. Then one of my friends gave me advice to create my own gallery, without using any existing CMS.

I decided to give this idea a try. Main thing is, that everything here will be made by me — art, texts, layouts, coding, maybe even programming in the future. I have a great chance to build my dream gallery for my art and writings and a lot of motivation to do this!

For a long time I thought about how to do everything in the best possible way, at the moment I had only layout coding at my disposal, but then I looked at the task once again and decided to design everything as I would like and enjoy myself, even if it does not look like traditional for the present time "business site-cards" and landings. Or 100% formal and official, at least for now.

Plans for the future - to raise my own level of knowledge in the direction of graphics and programming, layouts, construction and content of web resources. In one word — improvement only!
