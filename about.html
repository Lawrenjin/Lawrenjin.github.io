About this page

Also it's a good way to improve when you have something huge to work on. What do you need nowadays to build a real website? A little more than I have and know now yet with a complete lack of programming part, but I'm working on it.

Currently this page is buildt fully on HTML & CSS — and now is as simple as it could be because of need to be updated by hand. I will work on it little by little keeping in mind strong will to once create fully functional web-site from the scratch I have now.
